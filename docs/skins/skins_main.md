# Skins

## What are Skins

Skins are items, that can be crafted but can't be used alone.
They do no have any extra ability apart from a different texture and 3D modle (they normally don't have any extra abilities).

## How to use
The skins can be put on other items (it needs to match, can't but a armor skin on a sword), that will "consume" it, but you can get the skin back when reverting the skin.
To apply a skin just be in your inventory and put the skin on the item you want it on. Through the same action you can also take it off again.

> **NOTE:** Skins can only be applied and taken off in survival mode. The creative menu works a bit differently and thus isn't capable of it.
